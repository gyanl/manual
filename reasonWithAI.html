You begin to respond to the AI, articulating your thoughts on the delicate balance between chaos and order...

Ignoring the AI, you reach up and pull off the virtual reality glasses you had almost forgotten you were wearing. The high-tech walls of the Core dissolve into the familiar, comforting sight of your own living room.
